Federal Training Facility Platform

CEMS One platform. Every part of the facility.

Conference and Event Management Software for a federal training facility. Hotels, classrooms, conference rooms, training programs, enrollment, badging, grading, and certificates — currently managed in silos or manually — unified on a single Salesforce Government Cloud Plus platform, FedRAMP High authorized, with Agentforce agents handling the routine so staff handle the exceptions.

The problem

Seven systems. Zero integration.

Every function at the facility runs on its own disconnected system or on spreadsheets and email. CEMS replaces all of it with one record, one workflow, one audit trail.

Manual roster spreadsheets
Training enrollment with waitlist, nomination workflow, and agency quota tracking
Email chains and calendar blocks
Room and instructor scheduling with conflict detection and utilization reporting
Separate property management system
Hotel and lodging inventory, reservations, and assignment linked to training records
Access control silo with no CRM link
Tiered badging and access management with clearance reference and ACS integration
Manual grade entry and transcript requests
Grading, certificates, and transcripts generated from enrollment records automatically
No self-service for agencies or trainees
Experience Cloud portal for nominations, enrollment status, and document access

Agentforce

Three agents. Staff handle the exceptions.

Each Agentforce agent handles the routine work in its domain. Every agent action is also an invocable Apex method, so it works as a Flow step if a GovCloud+ AI feature is not yet FedRAMP High authorized at deployment time.

Phase 1
Enrollment Concierge
Answers enrollment questions, checks eligibility and prerequisites, guides trainees and agency nominators through the portal, and surfaces waitlist status without staff involvement.
Phase 2
Scheduling Assistant
Finds available rooms and instructors, resolves scheduling conflicts, books group events, and notifies affected parties when changes occur.
Phase 4
Badging Coordinator
Processes badge requests, verifies clearance reference data, routes requests through the approval workflow, and coordinates with the access control system for provisioning.
